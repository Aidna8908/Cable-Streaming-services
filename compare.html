Content Overlap Research

Are You Paying Twice for the Same Shows?

Research by Colin Dixon (NscreenMedia, 2021) mapped the overlap between major streaming libraries. If you have Service A, here's how much of Service B's catalog you can already access — meaning you might not need both.

"Looking at the y-axis list: 21% of Peacock's TV shows are also on Hulu. However, only 7% of Hulu's catalog appears on Peacock — because Hulu's library is three times larger."
— Colin Dixon, NscreenMedia, "SVOD Content Overlap Between Top Services" (Sept. 2021)
